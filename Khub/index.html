What's all this BetterDiscord stuff?

BetterDiscord is a Discord client mod which is made to theme and add functionality to Discord. This website hosts my best Bandaged BetterDiscord themes and plugins.

Why aren't you developing BetterDiscordv2 plugins?

BetterDiscord v2 isn't far enough into development for me to consider developing v2 plugins as worth it. Once BDv2 has been released into stable I will being to port my existing plugins as well as create some new ones.

Will any of your plugins get me banned from Discord?

Nope. All of my BetterDiscord plugins and themes are safe to use.

Why aren't any of your plugins or themes verified?

Me = Lazy

Ask a mod if you really want one of my plugins verified, but please don't annoy them.

Why won't GhostMessage ever be verified?

It "encourages spamming", and as moderators, the BetterDiscord Server staff don't want more work.

Will it get you banned from Discord? No.

If you abuse it, however, it will get you banned from some servers. Especially the BetterDiscord servers.

Why can't I use SafeEmbedGenerator on the BetterDiscord servers?

The BetterDiscord staff don't want people spamming embeds there, so I disabled it for those servers.
